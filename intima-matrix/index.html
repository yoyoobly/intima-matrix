Welcome to

Intima Matrix

a different kind of dating app

This app is about finding where two people match in their relationship with each other — by first answering the question: Who am I?

Not what you do for a living. Not what you look like. But the things that truly define how you approach life.

If we can't first see ourselves honestly, we won't know what we'll genuinely mesh with in a relationship.

The other things are superficial — so this part comes first.

Before we start

How Intima Matrix works

You answer honestly

A behavioral questionnaire about how you actually operate in life. No right answers — only accurate ones.

We find one match

One person, carefully selected. Not a feed — one real match at a time.

You talk first

Photos reveal gradually as the connection deepens — earned through engagement, not granted upfront.

No pressure, no feed

No swiping. No queue of faces to judge. One conversation given the space it deserves.

Setup takes 10–15 minutes. Progress saves automatically.
